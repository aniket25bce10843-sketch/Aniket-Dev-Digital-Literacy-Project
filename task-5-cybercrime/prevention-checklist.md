# "Stay Safe Online" Checklist
## 1- Enable Two Factor Authentication- Use authenticators which are app based for your university portals, emails and social media accounts.
## 2- Verify Before You Click- Before clicking links in your emails, check the url to see and check whether the domain is offical or not.
## 3- Secure Public Wifi Habits- Avoid accessing confidential information like your bank accounts in library open wifi.
## 4- Try to Set Social Media account as Private- Set your profiles in social media as private and avoid accepting unknown users follow request.
## 5- Permissions of Audit App- Regularly check which app has your access to microphone or camera or your contacts.
## 6- Regular backups- Keep your important work in an encrypted cloud service or an external drive.
## 7- UPI Safety- PIN Secrecy- Never enter your UPI pin to receive money because scammers often send "Collect Request"
## 8- UPI Safety- Verify merchant name- When scanning a QR code at a shop, always double check "Verified Name".
