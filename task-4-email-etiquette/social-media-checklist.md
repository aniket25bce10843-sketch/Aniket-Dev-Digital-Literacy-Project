# PART B
# SOCIAL MEDIA CHECKLIST AND REPORT
# Do’s
## 1 – Do use privacy settings to control who sees your personal posts.
## 2 – Do think twice before posting something.
## 3 – Do use Professional platforms like LinkedIn to build your networks.
## 4 – Do fact check the information before sharing it on your feed.
## 5 – Do credit original creators when you share their ideas or content.
# Don’ts
## 1 – Don’t post confidential information about your college or workplace.
## 2 – Don’t engage in online arguments with strangers.
## 3 – Don’t share photos that shows unprofessional behavior.
## 4 – Don’t use offensive language in comments or posts.
## 5 – Don’t tag other in photos without their permission.
